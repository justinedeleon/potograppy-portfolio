Welcome to Our Photography Portfolio

Capturing moments from today... Creating memories for a lifetime.

Photographer 1

Justine De Leon captures the soul of urban landscapes, from bustling train stations to gritty gas stations. With a unique eye for detail and a talent for finding beauty in the mundane, they transform ordinary scenes into captivating works of art. Their photography, featured in galleries and publications, offers a fresh perspective on urban life, inviting viewers to see the world through a new lens.

"Photography is the story I fail to put into words." - Destin Sparks

Urban Elegance

A striking image of a model standing against the backdrop of the weathered railing of a soccer field. The model exudes confidence and style, perfectly juxtaposed against the urban setting. The rusty hues of the railing complement the model's attire, while the blurred cityscape in the distance adds depth and context to the scene. Urban Elegance captures the fusion of sophistication and grit, creating a captivating portrait that celebrates the beauty of city life.

Midnight Oasis

A captivating night scene unfolds at a gas station illuminated by vibrant lights. A white car, headlights ablaze, is parked at the pumps, casting a soft glow on the surroundings. In the foreground, a motorcycle glides along the road, its presence adding a sense of motion to the tranquil scene. The blurred background lends an air of mystery, drawing the viewer's focus to the warm and inviting ambiance of the moment. Midnight Oasis invites viewers to embrace the serenity of the night and the allure of the open road.

Radiant Tranquility

An empty, brightly lit subway station in the Philippines. Yellow accents punctuate the clean, muted space, offering a subtle contrast. Sparse figures wait on the platform, adding a human touch to the serene scene. Radiant Tranquility captures the modernity and calm of urban infrastructure, inviting contemplation amidst the bustling city.

Urban Stride

A striking image captures a young man striding confidently down a city sidewalk. Dressed in a black "HUF" logo t-shirt, blue jeans, and checkerboard Vans sneakers, he exudes urban style and attitude. With sunglasses shielding his eyes and a serious expression on his face, he emanates a sense of determination and purpose. The low-angle perspective adds a dynamic flair to the composition, emphasizing the young man's presence against the backdrop of the bustling cityscape. Urban Stride epitomizes the spirit of urban youth and the relentless pursuit of dreams amidst the concrete jungle.

Urban Cool

A stylish young man stands confidently next to a chain-link fence, exuding an air of urban cool. Dressed in a white and blue jacket layered over a black t-shirt, he effortlessly blends comfort with fashion. His short dark hair frames his face, while sunglasses add a touch of mystery to his demeanor. Against the gritty backdrop of the fence, Urban Cool captures the essence of contemporary urban style, where simplicity meets sophistication in a timeless expression of self-assuredness.

Twilight Silhouettes

A mesmerizing scene unfolds as the sky transitions into shades of orange against the silhouette of wires crisscrossing the street. The vibrant hues of the sunset cast an ethereal glow, illuminating the surrounding landscape. The wires, stark against the fiery backdrop, turn black in contrast, creating a striking visual effect. Twilight Silhouettes captures the fleeting beauty of dusk, where the interplay of light and shadow transforms ordinary elements into a captivating spectacle of nature's artistry.
